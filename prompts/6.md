# Prompt

What else should be changed to improve readability? Please make a suggestion.

## Result

Top readability improvement to do next: replace all magic strings and numbers with named constants (and optionally a Coupon enum), then centralize thresholds in one section.

Why this helps:

Business rules become self-documenting, instead of requiring readers to remember what 15000 or FREESHIP means.
Future rule changes become safer, because you edit one constant instead of hunting literals.
The main flow in legacy_checkout.go becomes easier to scan.
Concrete example of what to introduce in legacy_checkout.go:

Coupon constants:
CouponSave10, CouponVipOnly, CouponBulk, CouponFreeShip, CouponTaxFree
Discount constants:
MaxDiscountPercent, VipBaseDiscount, PremiumHighDiscount, PremiumLowDiscount, EmployeeDiscount, BlackFridayExtraDiscount
Threshold constants:
Save10MinSubtotal, BulkMinSubtotal, FreeShipMinDiscountedSubtotal, VipFreeShippingMinSubtotal, PremiumFreeShippingMinSubtotal
Shipping and tax constants:
DefaultShippingCents, ShippingITCents, ShippingDECents, ShippingUSCents, USBlackFridayShippingSurcharge, EmployeeNonITShippingSurcharge
TaxITPercent, TaxDEPercent, TaxUSPercent, VipInITTaxPercent
